## 1. Scope of Services

**1.1.** The Developer shall design, build, and deliver a custom e-commerce platform (the "**Platform**"), branded as OrderMyPhones.com, intended for the United States mobile devices market with the technical capacity for international expansion. The Platform shall include the components, modules, and functionalities described in this Section 1 and in **Schedule A (Technical Specifications)**.

**1.2. Platform Overview**

- **Product Range:** iPhones and Android devices with dynamic, tier-based pricing.
- **Customer Tiers:** four (4) levels ranging from end consumer to wholesale.
- **API Integration:** real-time inventory aggregation from multiple external suppliers.
- **Responsive design** optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

**1.3. Customer Tiers and Pricing Logic**

The Platform shall automatically classify and upgrade customers as their cumulative purchase volume increases, applying the following tier structure:

- **Tier 1 – Consumer:** 1–10 units, retail pricing.
- **Tier 2 – Retailer:** 10–50 units, reseller discount.
- **Tier 3 – Multi-Store:** 50–400 units, distributor pricing.
- **Tier 4 – Wholesale:** 401+ units, bulk pricing.

**1.4. Supplier API Integration**

The Developer shall implement integrations with **two (2)** external supplier APIs to retrieve real-time product stock and information from external suppliers. The integration shall support:

- Real-time data retrieval (model, color, price, availability).
- Automatic inventory updates synchronized with the Platform database.
- Dropship fulfillment workflow support.

The supplier network covered by this Agreement consists of two (2) U.S.-based dropship providers and one (1) Dubai-based wholesale supplier for bulk orders, totaling three (3) supplier data feeds delivered through two (2) API integrations, as further detailed in Schedule A.

> **Note:** Any additional supplier API integration beyond the two (2) integrations contemplated herein shall be treated as a Change Order pursuant to Section 8 and may be subject to additional fees.

**1.5. Product Catalog and Shopping Experience**

- Branded landing page intended to establish market authority and acquire new clients.
- Filtered catalog with smart search by brand, model, color, price, and condition.
- 3D product views where source assets allow.
- Shopping cart with dynamic pricing applied automatically based on customer tier.
- Real-time order tracking and fulfillment status updates.
- Coverage of iPhone collection (including certified pre-owned options) and a multi-brand Android range.

**1.6. Admin Dashboard**

The Platform shall include an administrative dashboard providing the Client with:

- Customer management (tiers, purchase history, contact details).
- Order processing (status tracking, payment processing, fulfillment management).
- Inventory control (dropship API synchronization and owned-stock management).
- Price configuration (pricing rules per customer tier).
- API logs (monitoring of supplier connections and data flow).
- General reporting on sales, products, and customers.

**1.7. Customer Portal**

Registered customers shall have access to a self-service portal including:

- Order history with real-time status tracking.
- Visibility of current tier level and progress toward the next tier.
- Saved shipping addresses and payment methods.
- Account settings and personal preferences.

**1.8. Core Modules**

- **Stock Management** — track stock levels, monitor availability, and manage replenishment.
- **Payment Processing** — secure transactions, confirmations, and payment status updates.
- **Shipment Tracking** — order-in-transit tracking and delivery visibility.
- **User Logins and Authentication** — identity verification and secure access control.
- **Sales Management** — orders, quotes, and end-to-end sales workflow.
- **Customer Support** — centralized inquiries, cases, and service responses.
- **Analytics and Reporting** — operational reports and performance dashboards.
- **Inventory Sync** — alignment of inventory data across systems and channels.

**1.9. Technology Stack**

- Custom e-commerce solution with fully responsive design.
- Stripe integration with support for additional payment gateway options.
- Database layer with real-time inventory synchronization.
- Mobile-optimized front-end across all major device categories.

## Schedule A — Technical Specifications

This Schedule A summarizes the technical scope referenced in Section 1. The Parties may refine specific items in writing during the discovery phase without the need for a Change Order, provided such refinements do not materially alter the scope, timeline, or fees.

**A.1. Architecture**

- Custom e-commerce platform with responsive front-end.
- Backend with REST/GraphQL APIs, role-based authentication and authorization.
- Relational database with real-time inventory synchronization.
- Integration layer for two (2) supplier APIs covering up to three (3) supplier feeds.

**A.2. External Integrations**

- **Supplier API #1** — U.S.-based dropship provider.
- **Supplier API #2** — U.S.-based dropship provider and/or Dubai-based wholesale supplier (consolidated).
- **Stripe** — primary payment gateway, with architecture allowing future gateways.
- **Shipment/tracking provider** as agreed during discovery.

**A.3. Out of Scope (unless agreed via Change Order)**

- Native mobile apps (iOS/Android).
- Custom ERP integrations beyond the two (2) supplier APIs covered herein.
- Migration of legacy data from pre-existing systems.
- Multi-language and multi-currency beyond English/USD at launch.
